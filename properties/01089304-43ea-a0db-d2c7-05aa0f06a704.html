Additional Information

✦  Cash Or Hard Money Only

These deals are offered as investment properties and are often in very poor repair. As a result, the seller only accepts offers using all cash or a hard money loan. If you need a referral to a hard money lender, please let me know.

✦  Sold As Is

These properties are in obvious need of repair or renovation so the seller only accepts offers with no repair requests and usually require the inspection contingency to be waived at time of offer.

✦  Earnest Money Required

The sellers usually require the earnest money deposit to be sent in to actually secure the property. They are often first come, first serve. The first buyer to deposit the earnest money is the buyer to secure the contract.

✦  Shorter Escrow Period

The sellers usually have limited time to close, so time is of the essence with these properties. Once under contract, there is usually a much shorter escrow period compared to a traditional sale. It is important that much of the due diligence be done up front before getting the property under contract.
